Types of Robotices

It’s not easy to define what robots are, and it’s not easy to categorize them either. Each robot has its own unique features, and as a whole robots vary hugely in size, shape, and capabilities. Still, many robots share a variety of features. Here are the 11 categories we used to classify robots.

  • Aerospace
  • Consumer
  • Disaster
  • Response
  • Drones
  • Education
  • Entertainment
  • Exoskeletions
  • Humanoids
  • Industrial
  • Medical
  • Military & Security
